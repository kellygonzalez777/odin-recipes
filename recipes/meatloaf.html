Meatloaf

Description

This recipe I got from Recipe Tin Eats. I really enjoy her recipes and have included this to our weekly dinners. I am a fairly new cook and I really enjoyed this meatloaf recipe I got from her. I hope you enjoy as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup panki breadcrumbs
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 lb ground beef (mince)
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Meatloaf Glaze

  • 1/2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
