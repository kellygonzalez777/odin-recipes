Speghetti Bolognese

Description

My favorite recipe from Recipe Tin Eats

In my opnion, if you want something fancier than the canned spaghetti sauce this is your go to recipe. This is easy to make and much more delicious. I like making this when I have time on my hands and want a few days of leftovers. Yummmmm.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 lb beef mince (ground beef)
  • 1/2 cup beef broth / stock
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 28 oz can crushed tomato
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • 2 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 3/4 tsp cooking salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

To serve

  • 13 oz spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese
