Chilli Con Carne

Description

I got this recipe from Recipe Tin Eats.

I've never made chilli before this. This was my first attempt on chilli and my boyfriend and I were very impressed on how it turned out. This is a good meal to include with anything I believe. I made chili dogs with this and dipped it with chips as well! I also ate it by itself. By the way, I cooked it for 1.5 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1 red capsicum (bell pepper)
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 28 oz can crushed tomato
  • 14oz can red kidney beans (drained)
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups water

Chili Spice Mix

  • 2 tsp cayene pepper
  • 4 tsp parika powder
  • 5 tsp cumin poweder
  • 2 tsp garlic poweder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp cooking salt / kosher salt

To serve

  • Rice, corn ships tortillas
  • sour cream, yoghurt, grated cheese, coriander / cilantro
